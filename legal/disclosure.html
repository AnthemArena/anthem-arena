Affiliate Disclosure

Transparency in how we operate and earn from this site

Our Commitment to Transparency

At League Music Tournament, we believe in complete transparency with our visitors. This page explains how we earn revenue through affiliate partnerships and what that means for you.

Bottom Line: When you purchase products through links on our site, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you. This helps us maintain and improve the site while keeping all voting and content free for visitors.

Amazon Associates Program

League Music Tournament is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com.

What this means: When you click on an Amazon link on our site and make a purchase, we receive a small commission from Amazon. This commission comes at no extra cost to you—the price you pay is exactly the same whether you use our link or go directly to Amazon.

What We Recommend and Why

We only recommend products and resources that we believe will genuinely benefit League of Legends fans and music enthusiasts. Our recommendations are based on:

  • Relevance to League Music - Products that meaningfully contribute to enjoying League of Legends music and culture
  • Quality of Content - Official merchandise, music albums, and resources that fans will appreciate
  • Visitor Value - Resources that enhance the experience of exploring League's incredible music library

Our editorial independence: Affiliate commissions never influence tournament results, voting outcomes, or editorial content. Tournament winners are determined entirely by community votes.

Where You'll See Affiliate Links

We currently feature affiliate links in the following places:

  • Post-Vote Book Recommendations - After voting in a match, you may see a book recommendation modal featuring League of Legends novels and art books
  • Music Gallery Cards - Some music videos in our gallery include relevant book recommendations
  • Match Pages - Book recommendations may appear alongside match information when relevant to the featured songs

All affiliate links are clearly marked and directly relevant to the League of Legends music and lore being featured.

Other Affiliate Relationships

Currently, Amazon Associates is our primary affiliate partnership. If we establish relationships with other affiliate programs in the future, we will clearly disclose them on this page and mark affiliate links appropriately throughout the site.

Non-Affiliated Content

The majority of our content—including tournament brackets, voting pages, match archives, and music video galleries—contains no affiliate links whatsoever. Our primary mission is to celebrate League of Legends music through community engagement, not to sell products.

Your Support Matters

When you use our affiliate links to purchase products you were already planning to buy, you're directly supporting our ability to:

  • Host and maintain tournament infrastructure
  • Create engaging voting experiences
  • Curate comprehensive music video galleries
  • Keep all voting and tournaments completely free
  • Improve site features and user experience

Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about our affiliate relationships or how we operate, please don't hesitate to reach out through our contact page. We're committed to maintaining your trust through transparency.
