What We Are

Anthems Arena is a fan-created platform where the League of Legends community comes together to celebrate, compare, and crown the greatest music in the game's history through bracket-style tournaments.

From legendary Worlds anthems like Warriors and RISE to cultural phenomena like K/DA - POP/STARS, from stunning cinematics like Awaken to virtual bands like Pentakill and Heartsteel — every song gets its moment in the spotlight.

We're not affiliated with Riot Games, but we're huge fans of what they've created. This is our way of celebrating the incredible artistry, production quality, and cultural impact of League's music legacy.

How It Works

1

Tournament Selection

Season 1 features all 64 League music videos competing together in one ultimate championship. Future seasons may focus on specific categories based on community feedback and interest.

2

Seeding & Brackets

Music videos are seeded based on YouTube views to ensure fair, competitive matchups. We use single-elimination brackets to create dramatic, high-stakes battles from Round 1 through the Finals.

3

Community Voting

Watch both videos side-by-side and vote for your favorite. No account required — just click to vote. Voting windows vary by round, with live vote counts displayed throughout.

4

Bracket Advancement

Winners advance through Round 1, Round 2, Quarterfinals, Semifinals, and Finals. Close matchups create intense drama, while blowouts demonstrate clear community favorites. Upsets and Cinderella runs make every round exciting!

5

Champion Crowned

The last music video standing becomes Season 1 champion and enters the Hall of Champions. The winner will get special recognition and may receive advantages in future all-star tournaments.

6

Future Seasons

After Season 1 crowns an all-time champion, we'll run future tournaments with different formats based on community feedback. Category-specific tournaments, redemption brackets, and special events are all on the table!

Why We Love League Music

League of Legends didn't just create a game — they created a musical universe. Over the past decade, Riot Games has partnered with world-class artists, composed stunning orchestral scores, and produced music videos that rival anything in the mainstream music industry.

The Impact: These aren't just video game songs. K/DA - POP/STARS has over 600 million views and spawned real-world concerts. Warriors introduced millions to League through mainstream radio play. Pentakill released actual albums that charted on Billboard. Awaken showcased animation quality that set new standards for the industry.

The Evolution: From early champion login themes to full-fledged music videos with narrative arcs, League's music has evolved alongside the game. Each year brings new innovations — virtual bands, animated cinematics, live orchestral performances, and collaborations with top-tier artists.

The Community: League's music transcends the game. Fans who've never played League discovered it through music videos. Players bond over favorite songs. Worlds anthems become yearly traditions. This music has become part of gaming culture itself.

We created this tournament to celebrate all of that — and to give the community a fun way to engage with this incredible musical legacy.
